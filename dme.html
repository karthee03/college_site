Mechanical Engineering

vision :

To empower the Diploma students of Mechanical Engineering to be Technologically adept, innovative, self-motivated and responsible Indian citizen possessing human values and contribute significantly towards high quality skill in technical education with ever changing world of Mechanical field.

Mission :

Imparting total quality education to the students through effective teaching techniques to build critical thinking, problem solving skills and practical application skills and to make them globally competitive mechanical engineers.


Developing the students towards acquiring employability and entrepreneur skills in the fields of design, manufacturing and service sectors through industrial training and also make them prepared to take up higher studies.To provide the students with academic environment of excellence, leadership, ethical guidelines and lifelong learning needed for a long productive career.
